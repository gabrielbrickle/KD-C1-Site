Median Price Passes 200K in all Three Counties

October 13, 2016

In Market Reports

Year-to-date figures show 13,675 homes sold in 2016 at a median price of $240,000 in our area. Comparing year-to-date figures from 2015, sales volume has increased 10% and buyers are paying 6% more for homes in the region than they did last year. Inventory has declined by 19% over the last 12-month period, with 5,491 homes listed as “active” for sale in the Charleston Trident Multiple Listing Service (CTMLS) as of September 30.

Tri-County Overview

1,546 of the homes sold this month in the region were in Charleston, Berkeley or Dorchester counties at a median price of $246,087.

Berkeley County

400 homes sold at a median price of $206,583 in Berkeley County in September. There are currently 940 residential properties for sale in Berkeley County— 795 single-family homes and 145 condos/townhomes.

Charleston County

820 homes sold at a median price of $309,645 in September in Charleston County. There are currently 2,277 residential properties for sale in Charleston County— 2,997 single-family homes and 720 condos/townhomes.

Dorchester County

326 homes sold at a median price of $207,950 in Dorchester County in September. There are currently 821 residential properties for sale in Dorchester County— 748 single-family homes and 73 condos/townhomes.
