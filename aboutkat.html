Kat Drerup

How Can I Help You?

Real estate has always been a part of my life – for 13 years my family relocated across the country during my dad's tenure in the Navy. This life experience provided me the opportunity to understand first-hand the process of buying and selling a home.

From the age of six, I could point out the pros and cons of a home, and especially loved looking at model homes. When I show new construction properties today, the “new home smell” brings back fond memories of my childhood. I would bounce around in pigtails making suggestions like extending the porch so I could jump off the deck into the (imaginary) pool in the backyard. Clearly, my career path was pre-destined.

Although some of my ideas did not always come to fruition; my creativity, energy and perseverance still remain characteristics I hold as your realtor. I know my passion will anticipate your needs and exceed your expectations.

I know what it's like to move to a new city; to start over and search for a new hair stylist and dentist. I know what it's like to buy your first home and the questions that come along with it. Most of all, I know that even though you’ll be my client first, you’ll always be my neighbor.

Originally from Athens GA (Go Dawgs!), I am a homeowner in Mount Pleasant. My dog, Sullivan, is named after our favorite beach.
