Sellers

SELL MY HOME

You deserve much more than a sign in the yard, fliers on the counter and an occasional viewing. I work tirelessly to market your home with a tailored comprehensive marketing plan to fit your needs. With a degree in public relations and extensive knowledge on social media, I utilize the latest cutting edge tools and personal touch to get your home sold.

COMMUNICATION – A PROACTIVE APPROACH

  • Maintain prompt communication throughout the sale
  • Conduct comparable market analysis to properly price the house
  • Recommend staging strategies
  • Provide feedback from showing agents
  • Coordinate showings with homeowners, tenants and other agentsShare updates on market and neighborhood activity

COMPREHENSIVE MARKETING PLAN – MAXIMUM EXPOSURE & PROVEN RESULTS

  • Create a custom website with a unique URL to feature your house and share with buyers
  • Host open houses myself, so I can give the best data and follow up with buyers
  • Showcase the house “Caravan” where agents from my office tour your house for their clients
  • Create customized fliers and brochures inside the home for showings
  • Advertise online at PostandCourier.com
  • Feature in Homes and Homesites Magazine
  • Mail personalized “Just Listed” postcards to all neighborhood residents
  • Publish Post & Courier Photo Ad display featuring the home 12 times over a 30-day period
  • Market online on sites like Zillow, Trulia and realtor.com

Behind the Scenes – So You Don’t Have to Worry

  • Schedule professional photographer to take pictures
  • Coordinate closing process with buyer’s agent and lender
  • Measure overall square footage and interior room sizes
  • Research all tax, HOA and utility information
  • Work with buyer’s agent to schedule the home inspection, CL100 and final walkthrough around your schedule
